When can Taurus be seen?

Taurus can be seen in the nothern hemisphere between Fall and Spring, and can be seen in the sourthern hemisphere between Spring and Fall.

Story

In Greek mythology, Taurus is often associated with Zeus. It is said that once upon a time, Zeus grew attracted to Europa, a Phoenician princess, and the daughter of King Agenor of Phoenicia and Telephassa. Because of this, Zeus appeared in front of her as an attractive white bull name Taurus. Europa eventually fell for bull (Zeus) and climed on its back. Zeus then took Europa to the beach and jumped in the water. Finally, Zeus took Europa to Crete. There, Zeus took the form of an eagle and had 3 sons with Europa.

Interesting facts

  • Some of the also have traditional names. For exmaple:
    • α Tauri is traditionally called Aldebaran
    • β Tauri is traditionally called Elnath
    • γ Tauri is traditionally called prima-hyadum
    • The Taurus constallation has some of the the brightest stars out of any constallation. For example, Aldebaran, Alcyon, and Elnat are all in the Taurus constallation.
